Pizzagate Conspiracy

This conspiracy theory claimed that a Washington, D.C. pizza restaurant was a front for a child trafficking ring involving high-ranking officials of the Democratic Party. Fact: Investigations found no evidence to support these claims, and the theory was debunked as entirely baseless.

5G Coronavirus Conspiracy

Some claimed that the rollout of 5G technology was linked to the spread of the coronavirus pandemic. Fact: Scientists and health experts confirmed that there is no connection between 5G technology and the virus. COVID-19 is caused by the novel coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2, which is spread through respiratory droplets.

Finland Doesn't Exist

This conspiracy theory suggests that the country of Finland is not real and is actually a hoax perpetrated by the Japanese and Russian governments. Despite its absurdity, the theory gained a following online and even has its subreddit dedicated to discussing it.
